If I apply a force of +10 N to the right and friction acts with -10 N to the left, why are the signs important?

Alaa Mohammed • 1 day ago

The signs indicate the direction of the forces. A positive sign means the force is applied in one direction (like to the right), and a negative sign means it's in the opposite direction (like to the left). The signs help us determine if the forces are working together or opposing each other. In this case, since the forces are equal but opposite, they cancel each other out.

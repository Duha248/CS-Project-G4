News Title: UOB Introduces New Cybersecurity Program

College: College of Information Technology

Date: 05/03/2024

Author: College of Information Technology

News Description: University of Bahrain has launched a new Cybersecurity program to equip students with essential skills in ethical hacking, data protection, and network security. News Title: University of Bahrain Launches New AI Research Center

College: College of Information Technology

Date: 11/12/2024

Author: Dr. Ahmed Al-Khalifa

News Description: The University of Bahrain has opened its first AI Research Center to promote innovation in artificial intelligence. The center will support research in education, healthcare, and business, providing students and faculty with advanced resources. The launch event featured university leaders and industry experts discussing AI's role in Bahrain's digital future.

College: College of Law

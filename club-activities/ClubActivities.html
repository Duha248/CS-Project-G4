About Club Activities

At Campus Hub, we provide the ultimate University experience by encouraging students to participate in various activities.

Get to know our clubs!

Music Club

The Music club is all about giving the students who have a passion in singing, playing an instrument or even just performing a home.


Debate Club

The Debate club is for the students who love public speaking and want to improve their argumentation and comunication skills, it also help build confidence.


Chess Club

The chess club is a big community with top students that have advanced problem-solving skills, it helps improve your logical thinking and also helps you relax.


Sports Club

The sports club is for the students who love sports and want to stay active, it has a big role in building teamwork and getting to know new people.
